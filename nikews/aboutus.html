Nike - The story behind the swoosh

Founded in 1964 by Phil Knight and Bill Bowerman, Nike is a leading brand in providing sneakers and clothing for the world. Our mission is what drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential. We do that by creating groundbreaking sport innovations, by making our products more sustainably, by building a creative and diverse global team and by making a positive impact in communities where we live and work. Based in Beaverton, Oregon, NIKE, Inc. includes the Nike, Converse, and Jordan brands.
